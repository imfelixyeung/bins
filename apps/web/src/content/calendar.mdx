# Subscribing to your Bin Days Calendar

You can easily stay informed about your household waste collection schedule by subscribing to the iCalendar URL feed provided by us. This will allow you to automatically add and update waste collection days directly in your preferred calendar app, ensuring you never miss a collection day.

## Steps

<Steps>

### Generate the iCalendar URL

1. Go to the [home page](/)
2. Search for your address using your postcode
3. Select your address
4. Scroll to the bottom of the page
5. Copy the URL for the iCalendar feed

### Subscribe using your preferred calendar app

The setup varies depending on your calendar app, here are the guides for popular apps:

### Syncing and Updates

If setup correctly, your calendar will automatically sync with the iCalendar feed. New bin dates will automatically be added to your calendar, and existing dates will be updated as needed.
